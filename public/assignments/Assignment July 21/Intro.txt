Open the [Twinkle example file](https://ianhattwick.com/creativitas/Twinkle) and explore the basics of synthesis. Get a feel for:
* How the cutoff and resonance knob affect the sound
* How the envelope controls (attack, decay, sustain, and release) affect the sound
* how envDepth causes the envelope to affect the filter

* explore the different options for changing sequences.
